## Description

Learn piano notes by seeing, hearing, and playing them. Read the Staff, train by ear, and answer on screen or with a connected USB MIDI keyboard or digital piano.

Completely free and open-source. No subscriptions, no premium unlocks, and no ads.

## Practice Your Way

- Learn Treble Clef, Bass Clef, or both.
- Choose Natural Notes or Sharps and Flats across Octaves 2 through 6.
- Build Cues from one to seven Notes.
- Practice with Staff notation, Cue Sounds, or both.
- Answer using On-Screen Piano Keyboard or a compatible USB MIDI instrument.
- Follow guided Tutorials with hints, feedback, and celebrations.

## See Your Progress

- Track every Note by Clef and Octave.
- Adaptive Cues focus on Notes you are still learning.
- Practice any Clef and Octave directly from Progress.
- Keep everything on your device and reset anytime.

## Make Practice Comfortable

- Control Cue Sounds, Keyboard Sounds, Helper Glow, and Tempo Meter.
- Reduce Motion or enable Darker Practice Mode.
- Enjoy Dark Mode and responsive iPhone and iPad layouts in portrait or landscape.
- Reset Tutorials whenever you want.

## Made for Apple Devices

Built with native SwiftUI for a familiar iOS experience on iPhone and iPad.
